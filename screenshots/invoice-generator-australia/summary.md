## Final Comparison

**What we do better than all three competitors:**
- Animated invoice preview with line-by-line slide-in and total reveal — no competitor has any animation
- Paper-document styling with gradient top bar gives the preview a professional, tangible feel
- Finance-appropriate navy/blue palette (#0F4C75) signals trust better than RosterElf's green or Billdu's Bootstrap blue
- DM Sans + DM Mono font pairing is intentional — competitors use system fonts or generic sans-serif
- No signup, no cookie wall, no friction — Billdu has cookie consent, Xero funnels to signup
- ABN validation with ATO checksum algorithm — same as RosterElf, better than Billdu/Xero free tier

**What competitors still do better (notes for future iterations):**
- Xero has brand authority and trust signals we can't match yet (logos, testimonials, security badges)
- RosterElf has save/load draft functionality — we should consider localStorage persistence
- Xero's overall page weight and performance optimization is enterprise-grade

**Verdict:** Our tool is visually superior to Billdu and RosterElf. Competitive with Xero on design, with the animated preview as a genuine differentiator. The $1 PDF download moment is well-framed with the preview acting as proof of quality.
