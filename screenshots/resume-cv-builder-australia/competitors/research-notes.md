# Competitor Research Notes — Resume CV Builder Australia

## Competitor 1: resume.io
- **URL:** https://www.resume.io
- **Layout:** Marketing landing page with CTA-heavy hero, template gallery below. Actual builder is behind signup wall.
- **Colour scheme:** Blue/teal primary, white background, clean sans-serif fonts
- **What works well:** Strong social proof (millions of users), polished template previews, clear value proposition
- **What to avoid:** Requires account creation before you can build anything. Free tier is extremely limited (1 resume, no PDF download without paid plan). Dark patterns around upselling.

## Competitor 2: canva.com/resumes/templates
- **URL:** https://www.canva.com/resumes/templates/
- **Layout:** Grid-based template gallery. Minimal text, heavy on visual previews. Filters by style/industry.
- **Colour scheme:** White/light grey background, purple accent (Canva brand), minimal colour usage
- **What works well:** Huge template variety, visual-first browsing, recognisable brand trust
- **What to avoid:** Generic global templates not optimised for Australian job market. No ATS guidance. Requires Canva account. Many "free" templates are actually Pro-only.

## Competitor 3: novoresume.com
- **URL:** https://novoresume.com
- **Layout:** Marketing-heavy landing page with feature sections, testimonials, and template previews. Builder behind signup.
- **Colour scheme:** Teal/turquoise primary (#2FA89E), white background, blue accents, illustrations throughout
- **What works well:** Feature comparison sections, ATS-friendly messaging, professional illustrations
- **What to avoid:** Requires signup. Free tier limited to 1 resume. Heavy marketing copy before you reach the tool. Slow load due to illustrations.

## Three Design Decisions Based on Research

1. **No signup wall:** All three competitors require account creation. Our tool lets users start building immediately — type and see a live preview. This is our primary differentiator.

2. **ATS guidance built in:** Novoresume mentions ATS but doesn't guide users through it. Our completion score ring actively shows which sections are filled, encouraging thorough, ATS-friendly resumes.

3. **Single-page tool, not a marketing funnel:** Competitors front-load marketing pages before the tool. Ours is the tool itself — form + live preview on the same page, with educational content below for SEO.
