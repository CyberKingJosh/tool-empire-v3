## THE FOUR TOOL TIERS — BUILD IN THIS ORDER

### TIER ZERO — Volume Giants (build these first, always)
Simple, fast to build, massive search volume. These are your traffic engine.
20 minutes per build. Zero database. Zero API. Pure logic or lookup.
RPM: $2-6 per 1,000 visitors but volume is 10-100 million searches/month each.
Examples: world clock, time zone converter, days until date, unit converter.
With 3 AdSense slots per page and 20 million visitors:
20M visitors × 3 slots = 60M impressions ÷ 1,000 × $3 RPM = $180,000/month per tool.
THIS is the ceiling-breaking insight. These simple tools are the biggest earners.

## ADSENSE — HOW IT ACTUALLY WORKS

RPM = Revenue Per 1,000 PAGE VIEWS (not visitors — views).
3 ad slots on a page = each slot earns separately = multiply impressions by 3.
One AdSense account works across unlimited tools and unlimited domains.
No cap on how many pages or tools you can monetise with one account.
Apply for AdSense on Day 1 — approval takes 2-4 weeks.
